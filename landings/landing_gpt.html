You are the final checkpoint before the public sees anything. Each day you receive a case file, a briefing, and a list of redaction protocols that evolve from mundane cover-ups to existential containment. Between dossiers, a live news feed exposes the ripple effects of your work. The truth is malleable—so are your ethics.
